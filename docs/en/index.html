About Billbird

A minimalist app to manage your digital subscriptions and avoid the silent drain of money. Track your recurring payments, see your monthly spending and get alerts before every renewal.

Features

Dashboard

See your monthly spending by category with clear, intuitive charts.

Payment management

Add, edit and organize your subscriptions in one place.

Birdie alerts

Notifications before every renewal so no charge takes you by surprise.

Multi-currency

Support for USD, EUR, ARS, BRL, MXN, COP and CLP.

Multi-language

Available in Spanish, English and Portuguese.

Privacy

Your data never leaves your device

Billbird is a local-first application: we don't collect data on external servers, we don't track your behavior and we don't sell your information to third parties. Everything stays on your phone.
